About me

I've always had an inclination towards web development, even if I wasn't always aware of it. I can recall days when I explored and modified the code on my MySpace page and how I naturally found simple solutions to emerging challenges. While I had dabbled with platforms like WordPress and Wix, I soon felt the urge to go beyond and build a page the traditional way. That's how I came to design the page for 'El Taller del Ciclista Urbano'. Inspired by that achievement, I created this page, and soon after, I secured my first client. This entire journey, from rediscovering my passion to landing my first professional gig, happened in an astonishing span of just a month.
